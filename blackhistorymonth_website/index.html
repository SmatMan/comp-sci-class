Significant Figures in Black History

Alphonso Davies, pictured on the right, is an extremely influential professional Canadian soccer player.

Nicknamed “The Roadrunner”, Davies has earned much recognition for his exceptional pace, ball dribbling ability, and creativity on the field.

Davies has also scored the only Canadian goal in the history of the FIFA world cup.

As well as this, Alphonso Davies was the youngest player to join the Canadian men’s national team.
